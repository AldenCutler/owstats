# owstats

data analysis tool I made for fun to look at my Overwatch stats.

after games, it takes a screenshot of the scoreboard, splits it into rows and sections per player and per stat, and then uses OCR to read the text from each section

## Analysis Ideas

- [ ] what heroes, when played by my teammates, lead to the most wins/losses
- [ ] what heroes, when played by me, lead to the most wins/losses
- [ ] what are the most common hero combinations in winning/losing teams
- [ ] how well do I perform with certain heroes compared to my teammates
- [ ] when we win, how much better/worse do I perform than my teammates
  - [ ] which stats are most important to winning/losing
- [ ] what is the impact of different hero choices on the outcome of the game
- [ ] given a set of heroes, what is the probability of winning/losing
- [ ] given a scoreboard, can we predict the outcome of the game
- [ ] what is the relationship between different stats and the outcome of the game
